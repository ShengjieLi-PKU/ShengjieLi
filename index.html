Industrial Projects

Shanghai Putuo Smart City Project

This project serves the government departments of Shanghai Putuo District Urban Operation Command Center. With the goal of efficiently handling a urban governance cases, we have developed an urban operation management platform structured as "one base + one capability center + five major applications + N scenarios". This platform plays a pivotal role in connecting superior and subordinate departments vertically and bridging peer departments horizontally, culminating in five core capabilities and achieving a six-fold unifieds collaborative effect.

My Role: Data Scientist & Solution Architect

Anhui Wuhu Smart City Project

This project serves the government departments of Wuhu Data Resources Bureau. With focusing on enhancing public services and urban governance capabilities, we have constructed a city brain structured as "an urban operations center + three major support platforms + four major applications", establishing a scientifically intensive, open, and diverse "City Brain" ecosystem.
